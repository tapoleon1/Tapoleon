FAQ

How do I get started with using your products?

You can get started with us by creating an account on Cryptohopper. Then creating an account on an exchange such as Binance, Kraken, Kucoin. Now that you have a Cryptohopper account and an exchange you can purchase one of our products such as strategies, signals or a copy bot.

What exchanges do you support?

Our strategies support all exchanges, however we would recommend the ones with the lowest fees such as Binance, Kucoin, and Poloinex. For the signals, you can see which exchanges we support on each specific signal.

What is the profitability of your products?

The profitability of our products is different for each product. However, we strive to beat the market (which we consider Bitcoin) with each of our products.

Can I use your products for any cryptocurrency or specific ones?

While you can technically use our strategies for any cryptocurrency, we would recommend only trading those with most volume. For our signals, we only use specific cryptocurrencies which we mention in the description.

How do I set up my trading bot to automatically execute your signals?

To set up your trading bot to automatically execute our signals, you first need to subscribe to our signals. Then you can download our signal specific template and load it in your base config. Finally you need to enter the API keys to your exchange.

Do you provide any additional guidance or instructions on using your products effectively?

We have a few guides which you can find here. Additionally you can always reach out to us via Discord.

How much capital do I need to start trading with your products?

The starting capital depends on what product you decide to use. However, we recommend starting with at least $500.

Are your products suitable for both short-term and long-term trading?

Although we offer products for both short-term and long-term trading, most of our products are focused on swing trading, which can take anywhere from a few hours to 1-2 weeks.

Can I reach out to you for support or clarification on a specific product or trading-related questions?

You can always reach out to us via Discord, or through email at support@tapoleon.com

Are your products based on technical analysis, fundamental analysis, or a combination of both?

Most of our trading-related decisions for our products are done through technical analysis. However, we employ fundamental analysis in our cryptocurrency selection process.

Can I customize or adjust the parameters of your products to suit my trading preferences?

Yes! You can customize your base configuration for all of our products and tailor them to your needs. If you have any questions you can always reach out to us.
