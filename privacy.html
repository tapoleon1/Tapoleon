PRIVACY



This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Tapoleon collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the http://www.tapoleon.com website ("Site"). This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Tapoleon.



Personal Identification Information


We may collect personal identification information from Users in various ways, including when Users visit our Site, register on the site, place an order, fill out a form, subscribe to the newsletter, and engage in other activities, services, features, or resources we make available on our Site. Users may be asked for their name, email address, mailing address, phone number, etc. However, Users can visit our Site anonymously if they choose to do so.

We will collect personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily submit such information to us. Users have the right to refuse to supply personally identifiable information, except that it may prevent them from participating in certain Site-related activities.



Non-Personal Identification Information


We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our Site. This non-personal identification information may include the browser name, type of computer, technical information about Users' means of connection to our Site (such as the operating system and Internet service providers utilized), and other similar information.



Web Browser Coockies


Our Site, Tapoleon, may utilize "cookies" to enhance the User experience. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on the User's hard drive for record-keeping purposes and to track certain information about them. Users have the option to configure their web browser to refuse cookies or to receive notifications when cookies are being sent. However, please note that if they choose to disable cookies, certain parts of the Site may not function properly.



How We Use Collected Information


Tapoleon collects and utilizes Users' personal information for the following purposes:


1. To enhance customer service:

The information provided enables us to effectively respond to customer service requests and support needs.


2. To personalize user experience:

We may analyze aggregated information to understand how our Users, as a group, utilize the services and resources available on our Site.


3. To improve our Site

We continually strive to enhance our website offerings based on the feedback and information we receive from Users.


4. To process transactions:

The information Users provide during the order placement process is used solely to fulfill the requested service. We do not disclose this information to external parties unless necessary to provide the service.


5. To send periodic emails:

The email address provided by Users for order processing will be used to send them relevant information and updates regarding their order. It may also be utilized to respond to inquiries, requests, or questions. If Users choose to subscribe to our mailing list, they will receive emails containing company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. Users can unsubscribe from future emails using the detailed unsubscribe instructions provided at the bottom of each email or by contacting us through our Site.



How We Protect Your Information


At Tapoleon, we implement suitable data collection, storage, and processing practices, as well as security measures, to safeguard your personal information, username, password, transaction details, and data stored on our Site. We take precautions to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of this information. Sensitive and private data exchanged between our Site and Users is transmitted over an SSL secured communication channel. This ensures encryption and protection through the use of digital signatures, further enhancing the security of the information.



Third Party Website


Users may come across advertising or other content on the Tapoleon website that includes links to the sites and services of our partners, suppliers, advertisers, sponsors, licensors, and other third parties. It is important to note that we do not have control over the content or links that appear on these external sites, nor are we responsible for the practices employed by websites linked to or from our Site. Furthermore, these external sites or services, along with their content and links, may undergo regular modifications. They may also have their own privacy policies and customer service policies in place. Any browsing or interaction on other websites, including those that have a link to our Site, is subject to the respective terms and policies of those websites.



Advertising


Ads displayed on the Tapoleon website may be served by advertising partners, who may utilize cookies. These cookies enable the ad server to recognize your computer whenever they send you an online advertisement. The purpose of these cookies is to compile non-personal identification information about you or other individuals who use your computer. This information enables ad networks to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe would be most relevant to your interests. It's important to note that this privacy policy does not govern the use of cookies by any advertisers.



Changes To This Privacy Policy


Tapoleon reserves the right to update this privacy policy at its discretion, and any changes will be indicated by revising the updated date at the bottom of this page. We recommend that Users regularly check this page to stay informed about how we safeguard the personal information we collect. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to periodically review this privacy policy and stay informed of any modifications.



Your Acceptance Of These Terms


By using the Tapoleon Site, you indicate your acceptance of both this privacy policy and the terms of service. If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using our Site. Your ongoing use of the Site after any revisions to this policy will be considered as your acceptance of those changes.



Contacting Us


If you have any inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, the practices of the Tapoleon Site, or your interactions with our Site, please reach out to us at support@tapoleon.com
